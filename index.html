About Me

Hi, I’m Shamika. I am a graduate student at Northwestern University majoring in Computer Engineering, specializing in Artificial Intelligence.



Working as a Software Development Intern at Ugam Solutions, A Merkle Company during my undergraduate years at the National Institute of Technology (Nagpur), I gained invaluable insights into the ‘behind-the-scenes’ of software development and built an API for image enhancement. Further, I have developed several Machine Learning and Data Analysis models for use-cases all across the gamut. Currently, I am engaged as a Research Assistant at the Feinberg School of Medicine to develop Cancer Detection and Survival Rate Prediction Models for industry use-cases.



From writing my first program to training my first Generative Adversarial Network, my goal has always been to further my understanding of the workings of the powerful algorithms that effectively run the world and play a small role in developing them. Apart from living in a world of 1s and 0s, I also play tennis and spend entire nights with jigsaw puzzles.
